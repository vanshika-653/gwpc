Career Orientation Programmes

Demanding training programmes like Soft & Professional Skills training in the Institute through
experienced experts and programmes to orient students about industry expectations are organized.
Additional Expert/Guest Lectures, Motivational & Guiding session, and training sessions to improve
learning in the areas like Personality Development, Group Discussion, Aptitude Skills and Presentation Skills.

Industrial Training / Projects / Visits for the students

Industrial Training and Internships are the integral part of the study to acknowledge them for real world problems.
Students are placed at various industries for 6 to 8 weeks and under supervision and guidance of respective industry personnel.
Industry visits of the students with faculty members are organized to the relevant industries at various locations.
It serves the objective of exposure to large-scale practical applications of theoretical aspects for the students.
Students prepare the projects reflecting the needs and problems of the industry.
Institute faculty interacts with industry and students for successful implementation of such projects.
Apart from the above basic activities, Training continuously connect with Alumni present in the all
Industries for the benefits of students, Connects with Industries for Faculty Training and plays a vital role as
front of Institute to build a brand in the market & Industry.
