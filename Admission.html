Diploma Programme

Admissions to the diploma programmes in Architecture Interior Design And Decoration,Electronics & telecommunication
Modern Office Management, Computer Science & Engineering and Information Technology,
Medical Laboratory Technology,Textile design,Beauty Culture And Cosmetology
are done through Pre-polytechnic Test (PPT), usually conducted by PROFESSIONAL EXAMINATION BOARD (PEB,www.peb.mp.gov.in) ,
Bhopal in the month of April every year. The admission to diploma programme in
Textile Technology is on the basis of merit from the marks obtained by the candidate in the qualifying examination
(10th pass with min 40% marks for general category and pass marks for SC/ST/OBC category from
M.P. Board of Secondary Education , Bhopal or its equivalent). The admission forms are available for sale in all polytechnics
from 16th June and last date is 30th June that is sometimes extended. The admissions are open to bonafide resident of Madhya Pradesh
(except for wards of Military Personnel and persons displaced from
Jammu & Kashmir as per conditions laid down in PPT and Non-PPT booklets (online or print).
The college hours are from 10.30 AM to 5.00 PM. The details are as under:-

Course Fee:

1. The Tuition Fee for each diploma programme is Rs.7820/- per year that can be
deposited in two instalments at the beginning of each semester.Full and Half tuition fee
exemptions are available to SC/ST/OBC categories, Poor general category candidates, Gaon ki Beti, Vikramaditya Yojna
(For general category students on basis of 10th/12th marks) and under such schemes.
However, such students are required to fill up desired forms in time failing which full fee will be charged from them.
No fee exemption is available after course duration i.e. 3 years.

2. College Fee of Rs. 370/-(including Transfer Certificate Fee) has to be
deposited by every student in their I year and Rs.220/- for ever subsequent year.

3. Examination Fee and other university fees will be directly charged by Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal
through online process while filling up examination forms.
