Welcome To Government Women's Polytechnic College,Gwalior

HISTORY

Government Women's Polytechnic College (GWPC), Gwalior was established in 1994.
The first principal of this college was Mr. R.C. Parwar from 1994 to 1996
The college is approved by the All India Council of Technical Education (AICTE).
The College is committed to become a premier institute in the field of Technical Education among women.
The institute is adhered to the best academic practices and enhanced value addition and lifelong learning for its students.
The college offer various professional diploma courses recognized by AICTE with aplomb thereby linking the
women to the main stream of technological and socio economical development of the country


VISION

Towards a perfect polytechnic college with various diploma programmes and need based short
duration courses ensuring quality training through commitment of its members,state of art infrastructure facilities,
hard work, transparency and echo friendly policies resulting in employed youths and entrepreneurs for a strong society.
